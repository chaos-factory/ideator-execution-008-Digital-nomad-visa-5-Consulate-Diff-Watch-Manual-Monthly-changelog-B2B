About Consulate Diff Watch

Trusted intelligence for immigration professionals

Our Mission

Consulate Diff Watch was created to solve a critical problem facing immigration agencies, consultants, and law firms: staying current with constantly changing visa policies and consulate requirements across multiple jurisdictions.

Every day, consulates and immigration authorities update their websites with policy changes, new requirements, and processing time adjustments. Missing these changes can mean misinformed clients, delayed applications, or compliance issues.

We provide a single, reliable source of truth‚Äîa monthly, editor-curated changelog that tracks substantive changes to official consulate and visa program pages, delivered with source links and before/after excerpts.

Our Editorial Process

Quality and accuracy are paramount. Every entry in our monthly changelog goes through a rigorous manual review process:

1Automated Monitoring

We monitor official consulate and immigration authority websites across multiple countries and jurisdictions. Our systems capture snapshots of page content at regular intervals.

2Change Detection

When changes are detected, our system generates detailed diffs highlighting what has been added, removed, or modified on each tracked page.

3Expert Review

Immigration professionals on our editorial team manually review every detected change. They filter out:

  • Formatting or styling changes
  • Minor typo corrections
  • Seasonal or routine updates (like holiday closures)
  • Changes to non-policy content

4Annotation & Context

For substantive changes, our editors add context, categorize by importance level (important, moderate, minor), and create clear before/after excerpts with direct source links.

5Schema Validation

All entries are validated against our structured schema (snapshots.jsonl format) to ensure data consistency and enable easy integration with case management systems.

6Monthly Delivery

Curated changes are consolidated into a monthly digest and delivered via web, RSS, CSV export, and API, typically by the 15th of each month.

What We Track

Our coverage includes official pages from:

  • US Embassies and Consulates (visa processing, appointment availability, document requirements)
  • Canadian Immigration (Express Entry, working holiday programs, study permits)
  • UK Visa Application Centres (Skilled Worker, Student, Family visas)
  • Schengen visa authorities across European consulates
  • Australian and New Zealand immigration portals
  • Other major destinations and digital nomad visa programs

What We Don't Track

We focus exclusively on official government and consulate sources. We do not monitor third-party visa service providers, unofficial blogs, or forum discussions. This ensures the highest level of accuracy and reliability.

Who We Serve

Consulate Diff Watch is designed specifically for B2B users in the immigration industry:

  • Immigration Agencies: Stay informed across all jurisdictions you serve without dedicating staff time to manual monitoring.
  • Consultants & Advisors: Confidently advise clients with the most current information on visa policies and requirements.
  • Law Firms: Ensure compliance and avoid missed deadlines due to unnoticed policy changes.
  • Corporate HR Teams: Keep international mobility programs up-to-date for employee relocations.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Accuracy: Every entry is manually verified by immigration professionals.
  • Transparency: All changes include direct links to official sources for independent verification.
  • Timeliness: Monthly digests delivered consistently by mid-month.
  • Actionability: Clear categorization and context to help you prioritize what matters.
  • Integration: Structured data formats (JSON, CSV) for seamless integration with your workflows.
